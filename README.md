# Amazon-Sales-Dashboard

Amazon Sales Dashboard

1. Project Overview
- This Amazon Sales Dashboard is developed using Power BI to analyze sales performance, order trends, profitability, and regional insights.  
- It provides stakeholders with an interactive view of business performance and customer behavior.  
- The dashboard enables decision-makers to identify growth opportunities, monitor key metrics, and evaluate sales patterns over time.  
- It consolidates multiple data sources into a single, easy-to-understand visualization platform for better business intelligence.

2. Tech Stack
- Power BI Desktop for dashboard creation and visualization  
- Power Query for data cleaning, transformation, and preparation  
- DAX for creating calculated measures, KPIs, and dynamic insights  
- Data Modeling for building relationships between tables and supporting accurate analysis  
- Excel for initial data validation and preprocessing  

3. Data Source
- Amazon Sales Dataset in CSV format  
- The dataset includes order date, order status, sales amount, profit, product category, sub-category, customer segment, payment mode, state, region, and time-based data.  
- Data was cleaned, transformed, and structured to ensure accuracy and reliability for analysis.

4. Dashboard Metrics
- Total Sales – Measures overall revenue generated  
- Total Orders – Tracks the number of orders placed  
- Total Profit – Calculates profitability across categories and segments  
- Total Products – Identifies unique products sold  

5. Analysis Covered
- Sales by Segment – Understand performance across customer segments  
- Sales by Category – Evaluate contribution of different product categories  
- Sum of Sales by Sub-Category – Analyze detailed product-level trends  
- Order Status Analysis – Monitor completed, pending, and canceled orders  
- Orders by Payment Mode – Identify preferred payment methods  
- Sum of Sales by Month – Discover seasonal patterns and monthly growth  
- Sum of Sales by Year – Monitor yearly performance trends  
- Sum of Sales by State – Understand regional sales distribution  
- Sales by Region – Evaluate broader geographical performance  

6. Business Insights
- The dashboard identifies top-performing product categories and customer segments.  
- It highlights preferred payment modes and order status trends for operational optimization.  
- Monthly and yearly sales trends reveal seasonal patterns and high-demand periods.  
- Regional and state-wise analysis supports targeted marketing strategies and inventory planning.  
- Data-driven insights from the dashboard enable stakeholders to make informed decisions, improve profitability, and enhance customer satisfaction.
